Lion

Lions are the only cats that live in groups, a pride can have up to 30 lions.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Weight: 420 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 8-10 years
  • Habitat: Sub-Saharan Africa

The lion is a muscular, deep chested cat with a rounded head. Its fur varies from light buff to a dark brown. The males have a large mane whereas the females do not. They can be 63-82 in long and weight and weight 240-496 lbs depending on sex and geographical location.
